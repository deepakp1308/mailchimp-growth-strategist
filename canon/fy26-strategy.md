## Core thesis

Transform Mailchimp into a **global AI-powered marketing engine** for SMB and Mid-Market (MM) businesses. Serve as the **front-door to GBSG** by solving Grow Customer jobs, helping businesses grow revenue **2x faster**.

**FY26 focus:** deliver a compelling solution for **Simple Commerce (SC)** Grow Customer jobs and accelerate **AI / done-for-you (DFY)** experiences. Connected-platform vision will be clarified in H1 FY26.

---

## 3 priority customer targets (FY26)

| # | Segment | Size scope | Verticals | Value | PMF |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Subscription-based** | Mid-Market (up to $10M rev) | SaaS/Apps & Content (excl. ecomm/retail-services-with-subscription) | High | Moderate |
| 2 | **Community-based** | Small & Lower-MM (up to $5M rev) | Civic/Social & Institutions | Moderate | High |
| 3 | **Digital sales-based** | Small & Lower-MM (up to $5M rev) | Ecommerce & Omnichannel Goods Sellers | High | Moderate |

**Deprioritized:** Complex Commerce (Professional Services, Construction) — needs CRM hub before MC can serve. SC = 75% of paid users / 79% of revenue, 3× higher penetration than CC.

## Product investment priorities

### MM Subscription + Community (P1–P3)

1. **Account structure & billing flexibility** — identity mgmt (permissions, parent-child, multi-entity, SSO), billing infra (annual/quarterly invoicing, wire/ACH, contracts, price lock).
2. **Channel expansion** — WhatsApp international launch, SMS international (incl. short codes / standalone), social posts (LinkedIn, TikTok, IG) for retargeting.
3. **Integrations & data enablement** — unified C2 profiles via CDP, behavior/identity tracking pixel, API + dev portal, GA / FullContacts / Gmail sync, fundraising/payment integrations.

### Small & Lower-MM Ecomm/Omnichannel Goods (P1–P3)

1. **Personalization depth via integrations** — Shopify data activation, BigCommerce/WooCommerce parity, ecomm-specific data/tools, static + dynamic promo codes.
2. **Marketing ROI through reporting & attribution** — ecomm-specific metrics (GMV, ROI), attribution, predictive analytics for churn/upsell.
3. **Lead ads** — Meta + Google lead ads first, then expand.
4. *(Exploration)* **Retention & Loyalty** — loyalty/rewards, referrals, reviews/UGC, AI unified inbox.

### Segment-agnostic Agentic AI / DFY (P1–P2)

1. **Advanced campaign management** — dynamic email content, frequency caps, content-block reuse, multi-audience sending.
2. **AI agents** — speed-to-value (onboarding), productivity (content gen, channel-optimized templates, conversational lead gen), strategy (lead scoring, predictive churn/upsell, upgrade workflow recs).

## GTM levers

- **Scaled channels** — self-serve, scale partners, strategic tech partners (Wix, Shopify, Canva).
- **MM GTM** — references/proofpoints, agency partners, onboarding for **$299+ MRR**, 90d retention + NRR incentives.
- **Expansion sales motion** — starting with **SMS**, extending to other jobs.

---

## Revenue levers (FY26)

- **Shifting customer mix to MM** — capability-building improves serving/acquiring higher-value C1s (SaaS/Apps, Ecomm).
- **Improving churn** — FY26 builds on FY25 Moneyball; specifically improving MM retention via better needs-fit + better churn measurement (proven growth/experimentation approaches).

## Definitions to remember

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Small** | <$2.5M revenue |
| **Lower MM** | $2.5–$5M revenue |
| **MM** | $5–$10M revenue |
| **SC (Simple Commerce)** | 1:many, primarily digital — needs rich audience/behavioral data + omnichannel |
| **CC (Complex Commerce)** | 1:1, hybrid digital + offline — needs CRM hub |
| **Grow Customer pillar** | GBSG product-map area MC owns (Marketing, Website-via-partners, Content) |
| **HVC** | High-Value Customer — billable list >25k OR legacy list >26.6k OR audience limit >10k on premium OR >15 mandrill blocks OR >15k payg credits |
| **Specialist personas** | Growth/Performance Marketing Lead, Lifecycle Marketing Lead, MarRevOps Lead |
| **Decision-maker personas** | Head of Marketing, Head of Digital & Demand Gen |
